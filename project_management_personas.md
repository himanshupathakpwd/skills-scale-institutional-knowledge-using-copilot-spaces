## Project Management Personas Expanded

### Business Analyst
- **Responsibilities:** Gather requirements, analyze business needs, and facilitate communication between stakeholders and the development team.
- **Interaction Patterns:** Engages with stakeholders and project teams regularly to ensure clarity in requirements.
- **RACI Guidance:** Responsible for requirement gathering, Accountable for ensuring requirements are met, Consulted for stakeholder interactions, Informed on project progress.

### QA Lead
- **Responsibilities:** Ensure quality standards are met for all deliverables, oversee the QA team, and coordinate testing activities.
- **Interaction Patterns:** Collaborates with developers and product managers to clarify testing requirements and issues.
- **RACI Guidance:** Responsible for quality assurance processes, Accountable for product readiness, Consulted during development, Informed on release schedules.

### DevOps Engineer
- **Responsibilities:** Oversee deployment processes, maintain infrastructure, and support automation of development operations.
- **Interaction Patterns:** Works with developers to implement CI/CD pipelines and resolve deployment issues.
- **RACI Guidance:** Responsible for deployment tasks, Accountable for infrastructure stability, Consulted for infrastructure decisions, Informed on new releases.

### UX/UI Designer
- **Responsibilities:** Define user experience and interface design to ensure user-friendly applications.
- **Interaction Patterns:** Collaborates with stakeholders and developers to iteratively improve designs based on feedback.
- **RACI Guidance:** Responsible for design mockups, Accountable for user satisfaction, Consulted during development, Informed on project timelines.

### Scrum Master/Agile Coach
- **Responsibilities:** Facilitate Agile processes, remove impediments, and coach teams on Agile methodologies.
- **Interaction Patterns:** Works closely with product owners and teams to enhance productivity and teamwork.
- **RACI Guidance:** Responsible for Agile processes, Accountable for team effectiveness, Consulted on retrospectives, Informed on team health.

### Stakeholder
- **Responsibilities:** Provide input and feedback on project outcomes, represent the interests of the business.
- **Interaction Patterns:** Engages with the project team at milestones and review phases to assess project alignment with business goals.
- **RACI Guidance:** Responsible for providing requirements, Accountable for project outcomes, Consulted for feedback, Informed on progress and changes.
